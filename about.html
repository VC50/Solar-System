Near-Earth objects (NEOs) are small bodies in the Solar System whose orbits bring them close to Earth. They are categorized into Near-Earth Asteroids (NEAs) and Near-Earth Comets (NECs).

More than 34,000 NEAs have been identified, and their potential impact on Earth is closely monitored. Some asteroids, like Apophis and Bennu, have received particular attention due to their size and proximity.
